2025 JAIST-Vietnam Research Exchange Meeting

27 ~ 28 Feburary 2025, JAIST, Japan

Welcome to the Website of JVREM 2025

2025 JAIST-Vietnam Research Exchange Meeting (JVREM 2025) is an exclusive research exchange meeting that aims to recognize and highlight the achievements of early-career researchers from Vietnam who have completed their doctoral studies at JAIST. This invitation-only event provides a platform for intellectual exchange, collaborative networking, and the dissemination of cutting-edge research.

Designed to strengthen academic ties and promote interdisciplinary dialogue, the research exchange meeting will feature presentations, discussions, and opportunities to explore innovative research directions. With the generous support of the president Minoru Terano, international travel expenses for invited participants will be covered, further emphasizing JAIST's commitment to fostering global academic partnerships and supporting its alumni.

Important dates

  • 27 ~ 28 Feburary 2025
  • Asahidai 1-1, Nomi, Ishikawa, Japan
  • Lecture Hall (Information Science)
